## **Mr.Nadhif: The Autonomous Beach Cleaning Robot!**

This project introduces a smart, eco-friendly, beach-loving robot designed to keep Kuwait’s shores clean — all **by itself**! 🤖✨

![Kuwait University](https://img.shields.io/badge/Kuwait%20University-KU-blue)

### 🌟 **What It Can Do**

* 🏖️ **Sand-Sifting Mechanism**
  Digs below the surface to catch hidden trash that traditional methods miss.

* 🦾 **Robotic Sorting Arm**
  Picks and places items into the correct bin — like a tiny beach janitor with superpowers.

* 🧠 **AI-Powered Classification**
  A machine learning model identifies and sorts items into five categories:
  
  🔹 Valuable Items
  🔹 Plastic
  🔹 Metal
  🔹 General Waste

* 🧭 **Autonomous Navigation**
  Equipped with navigation algorithms to explore the beach safely and efficiently.

---

## 🎯 **Project Goals**

* Keep Kuwait’s beaches **cleaner, safer, and more enjoyable** 🌴
* Help recycling efforts through **smart waste sorting** ♻️
* Reduce pollution and protect marine life 🐢💙
* Minimize reliance on manual labor and outdated tools 🛠️

---

## 🛠️ **How It Works**

1. **Search & Scoop** – The robot sifts through sand to collect waste below the surface.
2. **Navigate** – It moves autonomously across the beach, avoiding obstacles on its way.
3. **Detect & Classify** – The onboard ML model identifies what type of trash was found.
4. **Sort & Store** – Each item is placed into its correct bin using the robotic hand.

---

## 🌍 **Why It Matters**

By combining robotics, machine learning, and sustainability, this robot offers a futuristic solution to beach pollution. It doesn't just clean — it **protects ecosystems**, **supports recycling**, and helps keep Kuwait’s coastline beautiful for generations to come. 💚🌊
